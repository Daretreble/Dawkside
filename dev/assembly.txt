To assemble the circuit with your Arduino Mega 2560 and breadboard, follow these steps:

Components Needed:
Arduino Mega 2560
Breadboard
9x 10k ohm potentiometers
Jumper wires
Circuit Assembly Steps:
Connect Potentiometers to Breadboard:

Place the breadboard on a flat surface.
Insert each potentiometer into the breadboard, ensuring that each potentiometer's three pins are inserted into three separate rows on the breadboard.
Wiring the Potentiometers:

For each potentiometer, wire as follows:
Connect the left pin of the potentiometer to the 5V pin on the Arduino Mega (this will provide power).
Connect the right pin of the potentiometer to the GND pin on the Arduino Mega (this will complete the circuit ground).
Connect the middle pin (wiper) of the potentiometer to an analog input pin on the Arduino Mega. Use jumper wires for these connections.
Upload the Script:

Upload the Python script provided in the previous message to your computer where you have Python installed.
Modify the script if needed to match the correct serial port for your Arduino Mega.
Run the script using Python. It will continuously read the analog values from the potentiometers and print them to the console.
Testing:

Turn the potentiometers and observe the printed values in the console. They should range from 0.0 to 1.0 as you turn the knobs.
Additional Notes:
Ensure that the potentiometers are wired correctly, with the middle pin connected to the analog input pin on the Arduino Mega.
Double-check all connections to ensure they are secure and correctly made.
